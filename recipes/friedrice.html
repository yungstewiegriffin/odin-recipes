Vegetable Fried Rice

Description

Are you wondering what to do with all that leftover rice from last night? Make vegetable fried rice! This quick, budget friendly meal will take you right back to the mall food court. It's nostalgic, it's delicious, and somewhat nutritious!

Ingredients

  • leftover cooked rice
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • 1 egg, whipped
  • sauteed vegetables of your choice - carrot, bell pepper, onion are all great options

Directions

  1. Heat butter and olive oil in skillet on medium high.
  2. Add leftover rice.
  3. Saute for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, just covering the rice. Saute for 3-5 more minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Move rice to one side of the pan. Scramble the egg on the empty side of the pan.
  6. Add vegetables and stir to combine.
  7. Serve alone or add a protein.
