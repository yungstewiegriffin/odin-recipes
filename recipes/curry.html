Chickpea Curry

Description

Simple and easy to make yet nutritious and bursting with flavor. This chickpea curry tastes like home. Make it as spicy as you'd like! It also freezes very well and makes great leftovers. Not to mention, it's vegan!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • cooked jasmine rice
  • half yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • curry paste
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • limes and cilantro to garnish

Steps

  1. Saute garlic, ginger, and onion in coconut oil until onion is translucent.
  2. Add curry paste, toss for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chickpeas, toss and sautee for 1 minute.
  4. Slowly pour in vegetable broth and coconut milk.
  5. Add soy sauce and honey to taste.
  6. Simmer for 30 minutes or until chickpeas are soft.
  7. Add rice vinegar.
  8. Serve over jasmine rice with lime and cilantro.
